About my school

The Vallauri Institute was founded in 1962 in Fossano and became independent in 1969. Over the years, it expanded with programs in Electrotechnics, Computer Science, and a Technological Scientific High School. Named after Giancarlo Vallauri in 1981, it obtained quality certifications and accreditation for professional training. It collaborates with companies and universities, offers evening and international courses, and excels in national competitions. It is recognized for its excellence and innovation in education. This all wouldn't be possible without a principal as good as our beloved Paolo Cortese.
